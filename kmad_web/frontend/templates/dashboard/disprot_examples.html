In this example the turquoise motif LIG_BIR_II_1 at the N-terminus is clearly conserved, but is not very well aligned in the Clustal Omega alignment. KMAD lines up all of its instances together. An alignment like this tells us more about the reliability of the motif prediction.

Protein EMB-1
In this example there are two incorrectly aligned motifs - one is the same motif as in the previous example, LIG_BIR_II_1 (now highlighted in orange) - and KMAD also aligns it better now. Second motif here is the TRG_ENDOCYTIC_2 motif (highlighted in slightly lighter orange) beginning in the first sequence at position 65 and ending at position 129. In the KMAD alignment the motifs form the left side are shifted to the right, which shows how well the motif is conserved.
